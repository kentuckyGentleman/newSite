How can we make your business more valuable and profitable?

Empathy

People care a lot about a lot. And because of the different worlds we come from, people care about very different things, at different times and in different places. Making an effort to get into someone’s wheelhouse gives you a hell of a chance to create something meaningful.

Context

Numbers can't always stand on their own laurels. It’s great to know what’s happening, but it’s hard to find out where you fit into someone’s life if you don’t know why and how something is happening. There’s no sense in telling a story if you aren’t telling the story where people actually are.

Value

“What’s in it for me?” Seems like a very fair question to be asked when asking for people’s time and attention. The saying should go, “Ask and ye shall receive, only after ye hath given a reason to receive.” Knowing what to say, how to say it and where to say it is essential to people giving a damn.
